Learn More About the Company

CSRtech Studio is a company founded by four technology and video game enthusiasts: Cesar Hernandez, Luis Garza, Adrian Rodriguez, and Pablo Manriquez. Their story began several years ago when these friends met while studying computer engineering at university.

Over the years, CSRtech Studio has created a wide range of games, from epic adventures to challenging puzzles, always seeking to innovate and surprise their audience. Their commitment to quality and excellence has earned them recognition in the industry and helped them build a community of passionate gamers around the world.

With a dedicated team, shared passion, and a focus on creativity and fun, CSRtech Studio continues its journey towards success, excited about what the future holds and the digital experiences to come.
